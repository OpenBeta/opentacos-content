# Description
A mostly vertical crack that rangers from thin fingers to fists.  Short and mostly vertical with abundant features on the face as well.  Crux is low.  A short but fun climb that stays in the shade till late afternoon.

# Location
Lively up Yourself is located in the "Eastern Segment" of Spring Mountain.  About 15' to the right of the arete climb "Spring Fever" and the large chimney behind it.

# Protection
Pro to 3", mostly smaller.  Cold shuts at top
