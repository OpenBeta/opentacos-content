# Description
Scramble up ledges, crank over a bulge (crux) and then cruise a ledgy face with good protection to a tunnel-like exit beneath a large chockstone. Good route for beginning traditional lead climber.

# Location
Left-facing dihedral with crack just right of Passing Lane

# Protection
Standard rack. No fixed top anchors.
