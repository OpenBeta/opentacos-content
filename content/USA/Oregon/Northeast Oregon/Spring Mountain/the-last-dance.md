# Description
Stem and chimney up the wide crack system, step right across the chimney to a ledge system then scamper up a beautiful exposed arete, moving left briefly two bolts from the top. Need a 60 m rope to lower from top anchors.

# Location
Starts in the offwidth crack 20 ft. right of Blister in the Sun.

# Protection
18 bolts to chains.
