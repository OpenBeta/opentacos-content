# Description
First pitch - Climb the crack and face to the belay for Welcome to Spring Mountain.

Second pitch - Climb the steep off-width to the top of the cliff.

# Location
The crack just to the right of Welcome to Spring Mountain.

# Protection
Standard rack, a 4.5" camming device is useful for the off-width
