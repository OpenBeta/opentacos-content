# Description
Climb straight up the middle of the slab, veering right to gain the ledge above the 4th bolt. Holds right of bolt line (below 4th bolt) are off-route.

# Location
Starts on small ledge 5 ft. right of Funny Trumpets Arete.

# Protection
4 bolts to rap hangers.
