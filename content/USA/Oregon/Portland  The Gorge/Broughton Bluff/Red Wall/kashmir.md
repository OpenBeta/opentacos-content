# Description
Linked up with Thai Stick or Classic Crack and then Red Eye, Kashmir offers a long route with a good pump.  Not extremely difficult for the grade, it offers good position and great moves on the beautiful orange buttress.

# Location
Red Wall.

# Protection
Bolts, but bring a #3 or #4 metolious for above Red Eye.
