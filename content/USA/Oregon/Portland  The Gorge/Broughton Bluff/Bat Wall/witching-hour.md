# Description
Stem your way up till your under the small roof. Go around or straight up and continue up the slab. Time for the crux and then sustained climbing to the anchors. Lots of crimps, jugs, and side pulls to get you to the top.

# Location
Next route to the right of Dark Arts.

# Protection
9 bolts with mussy hooks at the anchors
