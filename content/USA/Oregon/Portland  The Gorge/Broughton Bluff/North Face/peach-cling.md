# Description
A Great crack line 15ft right of Gandalf's.  Climb a stout finger crack to easy 5.8 climbing on a ledge.  Head out left above the bolted traverse to a small aid seam.  Very technical, Very sketchy, really fun.

# Location
15ft right of Gandalfs Grip.
