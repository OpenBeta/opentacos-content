# Description
Follows a chunky broken crack. Cruise up the first jugs until you come to an overhanging block. Pro is thin and reachy to place. Pull the cruxy move and step up to the bomber jug up high. Move left onto the ledge then continue up an easier but slightly runout section. Once you get to the ledge you can make a tenuous leftward traverse to the

Scott Free

anchor or (recommended) step right and punch it through the overhanging hand crack.

# Location
Left side of the Main Wall, in an obvious mini-amphitheater. It's the obvious jagged crack on the right side.

# Protection
Gear to 1.5" including offset nuts if you finish at

Scott Free

, cams from 2-3" for the extension.
