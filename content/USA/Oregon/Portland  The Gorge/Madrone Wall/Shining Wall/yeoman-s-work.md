# Description
This is easily top-roped after putting up

Beginner's Luck

. The sole lead bolt serves as a directional.

Climb the start for

Beginner's Luck

but before reaching the ledge go straight up a hand crack. This will end at a slight overhang where a finger crack goes up and right. Layback this to a strenuous clip and make the crux throw to a small edge. Pull up to the ledge and scramble to the anchor.

# Location
Right side of Shining Wall, in the corner

# Protection
Gear to 2", one bolt
