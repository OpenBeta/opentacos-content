# Description
Start as for

Paleontologist

up to the midway ledge. Clip the bolt here with a long runner then make weird, tenuous moves up to the clipping hold. Work your way up the technical face, using holds out to the right. Finish at the anchor below the "falcon ledge".

# Location
Middle-right of the Shining Wall's main section. Bolt line straight up after the

Paleontologist

overhang start.

# Protection
5 bolts and anchor with rap rings
