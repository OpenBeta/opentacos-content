# Description
An excellent beginner route with a little spice to make it memorable. Climb into the deep corner and either place gear early or step up to the first stance and plug a piece here. Follow the thin crack as it widens, stemming out to the right. There are some loose chunks in the broken cracks on the right face, but they are easy to avoid. Eventually make an intuitive, airy step to the right. Consider your second and place a piece before pulling on jugs to the ledge. A few more awkward moves take you up to the anchor ledge 10' above. From here you can set a top-rope for

Divine Wind

.

# Location
Right where the South Trail meets the wall

# Protection
Gear to 2"
