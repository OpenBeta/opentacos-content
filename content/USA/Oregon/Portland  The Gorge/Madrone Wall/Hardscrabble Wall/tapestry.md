# Description
Start as for

Divine Wind

, climbing the featured crack up onto the slab. Get a cam into the good left hand hold before making a move up and around the corner to the right. Step across to a stance below the overhang. Reach up and clip the bolt, then work your way up the arete. Make a hard reachy pull onto the ledge, then scramble up a couple more steps to the last fun move along the crack to the anchor.

# Location
The prow that juts out right where the "north trail" meets the wall

# Protection
Cams from 0.5-2", 3 bolts
