# Description
Route starts in a flaring slightly wider crack, comes to a stance below a thin crack with a bulgy overhang and then cuts left to the Sisters of Mercury anchor. Fun, just wish it was longer.

# Location
Just to the right of sisters of mercury. The climbers right trail comes up just a bit to the left.

# Protection
