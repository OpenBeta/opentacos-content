# Description
A rightward climb with a vertical ladder bolted in place. The feet are very comfortable along the way

# Location
Walking toward the Orange Wall, before you get to meat and potatoes, you'll see a short upward trail on your right towards to a short wall. Go up. To the right is a shorter trail whose end runs into this bolt ladder

# Protection
Two PAS to alternate recommended
