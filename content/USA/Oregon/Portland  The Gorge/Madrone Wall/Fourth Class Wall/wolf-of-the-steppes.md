# Description
Climb the very wide right-facing ledge that cuts through the rock from bottom to top

# Location
Walking toward the Orange Wall, before you get to meat and potatoes, you'll see a short upward trail on your right towards to a short wall. Go up. To the right is a shorter path. Keep an eye to the left for the jagged ledge that features the climb

# Protection
Several finger sizes from .0-.3 or just brass balls
