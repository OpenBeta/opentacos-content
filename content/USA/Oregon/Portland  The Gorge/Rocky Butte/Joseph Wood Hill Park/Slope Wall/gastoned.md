# Description
Start on two right facing crimps, bump up to the next two crimps, then raise feet and reach right hand into a knifeblade gaston. Get a high left foot And smear right as you reach for the top.

# Location
Facing the park the stairs walk past the small boulders to the next section. The problem is just left of the center pillar.

# Protection
Pads
