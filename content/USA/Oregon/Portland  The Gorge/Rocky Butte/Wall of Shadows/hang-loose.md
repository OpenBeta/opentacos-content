# Description
Start up Foot Loose and climb to the third bolt. Depart from the chimney by ascending the right wall on good edges past 2 more bolts and towards the arete. Sorting out the finish is the business.

After cleaning the route, one can rappel from the Foot Loose anchors.

# Location
Start on Foot Loose which is located just right of the small 3' cave at ground level..

# Protection
Camalot sizes 0.5 - 2

Small to medium nuts

Bolts
