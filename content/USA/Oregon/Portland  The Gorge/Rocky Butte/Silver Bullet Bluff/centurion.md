# Description
Centurion is the last climb on the wall as the hill drops off. Not a bad finger crack but it is awfully short and a little dirty.

# Location
Location information not given

# Protection
Nuts & cams to 1/2”. No fixed anchor. One can either use a tree or cut over to the Glenn’s Route anchor.
