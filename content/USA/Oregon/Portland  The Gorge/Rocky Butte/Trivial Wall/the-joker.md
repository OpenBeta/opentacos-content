# Description
Begin in the central handcrack of Trivial Pursuit, and then split off to the right about 10 feet off the ground, going up the obvious left-facing corner system.  Finishes just right of Trivial Pursuit and shares an anchor.

# Location
Rightmost route on the small Trivial Wall area.  Climbs the corner and the face to the right of the corner.

# Protection
Single rack to 2".  Small cams (aliens, TCUs) can be beneficial.
