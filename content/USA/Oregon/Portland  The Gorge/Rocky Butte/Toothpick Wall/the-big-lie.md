# Description
Look for the line of bolts up a unique left slanting rail and bulging headwall. The crux is well protected by the third bolt. Rap chains are not easily visible from the ground but are just below the big block near the top.

# Location
Central section of Toothpick Wall.

# Protection
5 bolts and anchor chains.
