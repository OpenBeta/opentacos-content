# Description
Dirty. This utilizes the corner right of Blueberry Face. Climbing this by going into the corner or dancing up the arete. Grade varies depending on what line you take

# Location
Just to the right of Blueberry Face starting in the corner. You could probably do this on TR, but would swing left into Blueberry Jam if you misstep

# Protection
Tree anchor up top
