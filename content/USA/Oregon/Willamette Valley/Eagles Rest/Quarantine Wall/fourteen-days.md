# Description
Begins on right side face on an arete-like feature. Lots of variety. Starts with a juggy overhung section which leads into stemming, face climbing with open hand slopers, mantles, and thin face climbing at times.

# Location
Between By Myself and Curbside Stickup, following the face up to the arete-like feature.

# Protection
Bolts to sport loweroffs.
