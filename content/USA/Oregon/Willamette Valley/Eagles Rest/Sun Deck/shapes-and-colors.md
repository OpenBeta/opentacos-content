# Description
Slab leads to big moves on incut pinches and juggy slopers, finishing on highly featured arete. Brilliant movement and position. One of the classic 11's at the crag.

# Location
Between Microdose and You Only Live Twice.

# Protection
Bolts to sport loweroffs.
