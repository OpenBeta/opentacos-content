# Description
Straight up through highly featured rock into a wide crack/dihedral feature fun and 3D movement.

# Location
Look for the wide crack about 15-20 feet up and follow the bolts that lead into it.

# Protection
Bolts. Steel carabiners on anchor. Lower off.
