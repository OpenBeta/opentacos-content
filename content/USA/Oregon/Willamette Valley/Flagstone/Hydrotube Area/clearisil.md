# Description
Climb the common start of Acne Problem, at first bolt look left and take first of two bolt lines beneath a set of visible chains. Climb up and slight left up a slab to a thin section just as the wall steepens (crux). Pitch two is 5.8 slab up to the chains. Two rope rappel, or walk off.
