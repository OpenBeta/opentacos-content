# Description
Start on Fragile Life and climb through low crux to the ledge (bolts 6/7).  Traverse left into Joy Luck Club, do a few moves at the bulge, and clip the bolt with a fixed chain.  Finish Joy Luck Club.

# Location
Fragile Life and Joy Luck Club are the two right most routes on the wall that start from the ground rather than the slab.

# Protection
Bolts
