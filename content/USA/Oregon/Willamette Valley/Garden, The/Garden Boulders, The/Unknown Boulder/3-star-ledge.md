# Description
Stand Start, Make a big move to a good hold then a tricky reach to the top.

# Location
From the road, take the Main Trail (toilet bowl boulder trail), turn left at the "Boys in the Woods" boulder. If you are at the methlab boulder you've gone too far!

# Protection
1-2 Pads
