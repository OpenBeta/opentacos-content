# Description
Start holds are 3-5 ft to the right of Octernal's right hand start hold. Sit start on a left facing diagonal crimp, a right hand facing sidepull and a high left heel hook. Finesse your way up to a good horizontal crimp, then out right to a vertical rail/flake, good holds from there to the top.

# Location
Around the corner to the right of Octernal.

# Protection
Lots and lots of pads.
