# Description
Sit start on a small side pull and an edge on the underside. Complete several techincal moves to a small crimp. Fire to the sloping jug on the lip. The crux is hitting the lip hold without cutting, as well as keeping your fingers from bleeding on the crimp.

# Location
Shares the left side of the same boulder as The Ear on the Fight Club boulder.

# Protection
Several pads, the more the merrier.
