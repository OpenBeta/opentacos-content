# Description
Classic gardens warm up. Stand start under a large rail.

# Location
Around the corner to the right of the Siren, This climb is easily identified by the large rail ~7ft off the ground. If you take the trail in from the forest road this is the first climb that you will see.

# Protection
pads (one is sufficient, but more is better)
