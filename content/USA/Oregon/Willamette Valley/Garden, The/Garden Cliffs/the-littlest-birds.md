# Description
This is an extension to castle black. Climb Castle Black through the crux then traverse right to the bolt line. Â There are several cruxes. The first is getting from the ledge to the arete; then, there are a few others on the way up. Climb up the arete trending right. The anchors are now easy to see as they were extended over the lip with chains.

With the bolting of Castle Black this route is now g.

# Location
Goes right from about 3/4 the way up castle black.

# Protection
~10 draws. Rap rings at the top.
