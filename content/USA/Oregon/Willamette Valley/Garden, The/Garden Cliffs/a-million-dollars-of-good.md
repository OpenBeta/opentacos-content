# Description
Starts up the middle of the flake then walk left across the top of the flake to just left of the open book and weave your way up and slightly right. There is an alternative start to the left as different sides are randomly wet.

# Location
Walk to the platforms built at the far end of the cliffs. Starts in the middle of the giant flake and goes up to the left of the open book that separates the slabs

# Protection
Bolts (8?) and quick clips
