# Description
Climb the crack on the right side of the second column. Jam the crack proper, the face of the second column is fair game.

# Location
Main buttress area of the formation.

This route is listed as #26 on the Skinner Butte sign board.

# Protection
Cams and nuts.

Chain anchors on top.
