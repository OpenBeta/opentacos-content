# Description
Ascent the face of the column immediately left of bat crack. This is a face climb so as tempting as it may be the crack to your right is OFF! No hand jams, no fist jams.

# Location
Column left of bat crack.

This route is listed as #16 on the Skinner Butte sign board.

# Protection
Anchors at top.
