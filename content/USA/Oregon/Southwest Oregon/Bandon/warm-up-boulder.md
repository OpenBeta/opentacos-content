# Description
This is the ideal warm up boulder with good slopers, jugs and even some crimps to warm up on, start on the ledge facing the ocean and work your way around the right side to better jugs and then top out.

# Location
Hang ten and straddleshpere boulder can be seen through the crack to the right, this boulder is pinched between two much larger boulders facing the ocean
