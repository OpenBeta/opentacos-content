# Description
Begins at a bolt located about 40 feet west of a large snag or its remnants.

Climbs the buttress as directly as possible placing gear,climbing becomes easier as you go.

Rappel from 2 bolt belay (approx. 90 feet).

Refer: Rock Climbing Western Oregon Volume 3: Rogue topo B page 110.

# Location
Rabbit Ear's East Ear.

Refer: Rock Climbing Western Oregon Volume 3: Rogue maps pages 106 and 107.

# Protection
gear, everal bolts and bolted rappel.
