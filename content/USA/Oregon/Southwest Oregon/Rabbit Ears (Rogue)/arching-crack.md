# Description
Climb the open-book using the crack for protection (5.9). Follow the Arching Crack past the chimney, then traverse below the roof and out to the Slippery Gold first pitch top anchors. There is a 5.10c crux involving undercling at the off-width chimney.

# Location
Open book crack located on the right side of Rabbit Ear's Lower Apron.

Refer: Rabbit Ear's Lower apron. Rock Climbing Western Oregon Volume 3: Rogue maps pages 106 and 107.

# Protection
gear, bolted top anchor.
