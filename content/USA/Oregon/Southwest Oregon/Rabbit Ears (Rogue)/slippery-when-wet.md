# Description
First bolt line left of the large watermark.

Crux is a slick 10 foot slab section beginning at the 2nd bolt.

Rappel of rappel anchors or scramble off up low angle slab.

Rock Climbing Western Oregon Volume 3: Rogue. topo C page 110..

Rabbit Ear's Lower apron. Rock Climbing Western Oregon Volume 3: Rogue. maps pages 106 and 107.
