# Description
Starts between the dark patina of Studio Tan and Sunrise Tower. Follow bolts for 25 feet where route splits to the left for easy access to Studio Tan top anchors or to the right for easy access to Sunrise Tower top anchors.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua 2007, topo page 46 and topo C page 50.

# Location
Sun Bowl. Lighter rock between the middle tower patina and Sunrise Tower patina.

# Protection
bolts and chains
