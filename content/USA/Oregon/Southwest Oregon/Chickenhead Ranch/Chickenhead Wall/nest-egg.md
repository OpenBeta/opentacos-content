# Description
Fun face climb on chickenheads passing the two white bowls.  Stay right to bypass raptor nest, avoid during raptor nesting season.  Clean rock to finish right of small cedar tree.  Anchors on top, easy toprope access around left side of crag.

# Location
Starts 20' left of Black Chimney near dead madrone.  Toprope anchors offer other lines.

# Protection
Anchors.
