# Description
Really steep moves up the left side of the aqua cave lead to a crux at the lip. Pull yourself over and walk up the slab to the anchors up above.

# Location
There is a steep cave near the low point of the wall. The bolt line is the left-most one on the cave

# Protection
4 bolts. Anchor with chains
