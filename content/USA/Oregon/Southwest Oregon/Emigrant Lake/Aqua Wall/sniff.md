# Description
Follow Gloo, but go right after the third bolt, and then follow the lip. Pull over the lip at the end of the cave to the anchor above.

# Location
Gloo begins on the right side of the Aqua Cave. The bolt line goes right at the lip. See topo for clarification on the line.

# Protection
Protected by 5 bolts. Anchor with chains
