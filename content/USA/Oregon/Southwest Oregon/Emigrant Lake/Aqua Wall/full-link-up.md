# Description
The hardest route at Emigrant Lake. This route connects the lines of Devastator and Sniff. After traversing across the entire lip of the Aqua Cave, pull over the lip and end at the anchor for all the routes in the Aqua Cave.

# Location
Route begins at Devastator, which is the left bolt line of the Aqua cave. See topo for clarification on the line

# Protection
7 bolts. Anchor with chains
