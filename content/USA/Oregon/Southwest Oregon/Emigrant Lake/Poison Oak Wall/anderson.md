# Description
A good warm up or learning route with easy moves with lots of good holds. If you go to the right, the bolts can be a bit of a reach, but a variety of cracks and ledges provide good stability. 3 bolts

# Location
The belay station is located on the left of the Poison Oak Wall, just before the trail becomes quite steep up to the chimney at the end of the crag.

# Protection
Route is protected by 3 bolts. The anchor has rap rings
