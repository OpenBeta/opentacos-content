# Description
Easy start with a slopy middle. This section requires a lot of balance. The face at the end is an enjoyable challenge for the end of a solid route.

# Location
The beginning of the route is just below the small tree near the wall. The climb begins just before some "steps" on the trail as it goes along the side of the tree.

# Protection
Protected by 5 bolts. Anchor consists of 2 bolts
