# Description
Low angle on bottom, with a steeper face up top. Friction with horizontal cracks. The furthest left route on the Poison Oak Wall, and also the shortest. Watch out for poison oak!

# Location
The furthest route to the left on Poison Oak wall. The start of the route is near the top of the steep scramble, so belaying can be difficult.

# Protection
Protected by 3 bolts. Anchor has two bolts with rap rings
