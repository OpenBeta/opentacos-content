# Description
Difficult route with 2 roof problems that are also cruxes. Don't stray too far left on the first roof for the full grade. Caution: do not miss the fourth bolt!

# Location
Furthest right route on the Poison Oak Wall. A sloped surface makes it difficult but not impossible to belay. Route begins below the roof at the right end of the wall.

# Protection
Protected by 5 bolts. Anchor consists of 2 bolts with rap rings
