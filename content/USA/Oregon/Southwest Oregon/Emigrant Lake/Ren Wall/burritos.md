# Description
Rather vertical climb that has numerous chalked up holds that can easily get you lost. After scaling the face at the start, pull yourself from under a roof to finish the climb

# Location
Begins at a nice base on top of a boulder just above the high water line. The face at the start of the climb is much darker compared to the surrounding rock.

# Protection
Protected by 4 bolts, anchor with rap chains
