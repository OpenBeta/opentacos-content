# Description
This route is a continuation of Wimpy. After climbing past the chains, head for the roof and go straight up it, following the bolt line to the right. Once over the roof, go straight up for the anchor. The roof is reminiscent of a Gunks Roof

# Location
Begins at the top of Wimpy

# Protection
3 bolts from the top of Wimpy, 7 from the base of the wall. Anchor with chains
