# Description
This route starts with three bolts worth of easy climbing followed by three bolts that that get steeper and harder as you approach the crux at the last bolt.

# Location
Just uphill from 'Lady in Red'. The lowest (farthest right) of Jane's west-facing climbs.

# Protection
Six bolts. Rap hangers.
