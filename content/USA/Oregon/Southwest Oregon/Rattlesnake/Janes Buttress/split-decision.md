# Description
Easy climbing on flakes and jugs. The midway crux features small holds and thin feet on cool brown varnish. The finishing slab goes two different ways.

# Location
The front side of Jane's Buttress. Look for the chains 3/4 up the cliff.

# Protection
Bolts. Chains on top.
