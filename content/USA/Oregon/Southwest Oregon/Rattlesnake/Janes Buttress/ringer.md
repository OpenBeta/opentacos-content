# Description
Climb some sandy and mossy rock to a ledge. Then fun climbing on steep rock leads to a crux slab below the anchor. Moss, tree sap, and pine needles may be present depending on the season.

# Location
Next route uphill from Charybdis. Just right of a small cave.

# Protection
5 bolts. Rap hangers.
