# Description
Vertical start with very small edges. After the 4th clip the climbing eases to the anchor.  Chains hang from the overhung scoop but are reachable from the dish below.  Upper slab has the typical probability of moss and tree sap.

# Location
Second to last route when walking up between Jane's and the Dominator Wall.  One route downhill of Janeviore Shares a belay pad with Ringer, starts on the other side of the crack/cave, opposite side of the butters from Wedgie.

# Protection
Bolts, chain anchor.
