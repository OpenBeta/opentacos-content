Short climb (even for this wall) with a fun crux sequence at the second bolt. Easier climbing takes you to an anchor below the top of the wall.

# Location
Just outside the main part of the cave on the right wall. Starts between the tree and the wall.

# Protection
3 bolts. Chain anchor.
