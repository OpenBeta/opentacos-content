# Description
This climb follows the crack on the right side of the large slot outside the cathedral. A few bolts protect the start before a thin crack allows space for a few gear placements. At the top of the slot, you can climb right to then reach the anchors out left.

# Location
Begins on the right side of the large slot just outside the cathedral

# Protection
Protected by 4 bolts, gear to 1 inch. Anchor with chains
