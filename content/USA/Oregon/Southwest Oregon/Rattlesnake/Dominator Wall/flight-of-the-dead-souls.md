# Description
Cruxy moves down low on friable rock lead to easier climbing for about 30 feet. The last four bolts of climbing go up a pumpy headwall. Be sure to take in the view before you lower off.

# Location
The main Dominatior. Just right of the big gully.

# Protection
14 bolts. Metolius rap hangers on top.
