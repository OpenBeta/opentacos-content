# Description
Route starts nose to rock on the largest (lowest) column.  Big moves and bear hugs up the column lead to the first ledge.  Big moves or a dyno gains the second ledge.  Mantle and follow the column up to the face following the crack to the left.  Finished at the top with “Iron Man” at bolted anchors, 2 bolts.

(Use a 240cm sling to extend anchor over the ledge and reduce rope drag for TR)
