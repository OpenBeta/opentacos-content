# Description
Start below the flaring crack right of “Ajax”. Climb up to the “razor rock”(left).  This is the crux.  A variation of solutions pull through the overhanging cracks and finish on the slab up top.  Shares face bolted anchors with “Boner Direct”, 2 bolts.

(Rope drag from a TR setup is unavoidable.)

Standard rack.
