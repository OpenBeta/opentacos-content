# Description
Obvious overhanging broken column to the left of Ajax.  Protects with a #1-2 cam in the crack right of the broken column.  Pull the thin shallow crack using the arete to the left and move past the crux overhang to the face left of the broken column.  Protect face with small cams or nuts moving right up the slab and finish at a bolted anchor shared with “Ajax”, 2 bolts right of the final arete move.
