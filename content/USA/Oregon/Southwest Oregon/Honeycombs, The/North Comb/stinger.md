# Description
Starts out moving up large hand and footholds, then transition onto a face. Maintain your balance as you push and pull over bulges to the top

# Location
Located on the East side of the North Comb. The climb begins from a belay platform in the series there, and is the third route from the right on the wall.

# Protection
Protected by 8 bolts. Anchor with chains
