# Description
This route starts on a lower angle ramp with easy 5.5 climbing, then transitions to technical face moves over bulges. The route progressively gets more difficult until you Crest over the final bulge.

# Location
The furthest left route on the East Side of the North Comb. The climb begins at the lowest belay ledge in the series along the wall.

# Protection
Protected by 8 bolts. Anchor with chains
