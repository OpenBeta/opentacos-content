# Description
One of the longer routes on this wall. Starts out steep and strenuous, the first two bolts are clear to protect the crux. Some slab leads to mantling over bulges and transitions to face climb with more precise footwork all the way to the anchor

# Location
Located on the East Side of the North Comb. The climb begins on the series of belay stations, and is the second bolted route from the right.

# Protection
Protected by 11 bolts. Anchor with chains
