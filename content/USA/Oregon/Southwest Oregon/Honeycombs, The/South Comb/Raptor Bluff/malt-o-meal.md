# Description
Left of two bolted lines with same start.

# Location
From right side of Raptor Bluff locate rappel chains that take you to the base of Malt-O-Meal and Breakfast-O-Champions or access the bottom by way of the Clock Tower trail.

# Protection
bolts and rappel chains
