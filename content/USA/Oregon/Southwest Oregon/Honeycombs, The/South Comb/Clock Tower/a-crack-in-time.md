# Description
Belay from a large burned snag at the top of the Clock Tower saddle. begin climbing from the bottom of the wall up to the first of two bolts about 8 ft up the wall (5.5). Follow the thin crack (5.8-) to a shoulder and headwall about 8 feet from the summit. Climb the headwall (1 bolt) to the summit (5.7).

# Location
From uphill saddle of the Clock Tower look about 20 feet around the left side to find a thin crack with a bolt placed about 15 feet up.

# Protection
2 bolts, nuts and cams 2 inches
