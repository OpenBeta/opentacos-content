# Description
Begins with about 20 feet of slightly overhanging 5.10d becoming much easier 5.9 climbing through the top half of the climb.

# Location
Rappel 100 feet to large sloping ledge just above the bouldering tube road from first set of anchors to left as you top out on Easy Knot.
