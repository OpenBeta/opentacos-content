# Description
Tower Rotation is a Top Rope route just to the right of Candy Shop and Atrus Leap. A bolted anchor on top makes it easy to set up a TR after leading one of the other routes. Follow the dark face to the top.

# Location
Top anchors are just to the right of Atrus Leap. You can access them by leading Candy Shop or Atrus Leap.

# Protection
Bolted Anchor
