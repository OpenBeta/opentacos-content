# Description
Fun bolted climb with three distinct sections. Steep (for basalt) jugs to the 4th bolt. Edges on low angle slab in the middle. Finish on the arete. Watch for rubble on the ledge near the top.

# Location
Second bolted route from the left edge of the crag. Right of the Marge column. Left of the "Boys" slab.

# Protection
Bolts.
