# Description
Slab traverse following path of least resistance along top of the overhanging headwall from the HangTen belay (top of pitch-2) to Easy Street (belay anchors on top pitch-3).

# Location
Begins from the HangTen Belay.

# Protection
Unprotected except 1 bolt just out of HangTen belay.
