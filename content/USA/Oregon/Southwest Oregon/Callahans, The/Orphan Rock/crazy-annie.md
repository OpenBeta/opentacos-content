# Description
Climbs right side of the Orphan just right of the upper saddle ridge. Top rope from Jacob's Ladder top anchors through a series of huecos. Does not top out.

Refer to Topo Ad pp. 153.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon (2007) pp 66-67, 152-153.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion top anchors
