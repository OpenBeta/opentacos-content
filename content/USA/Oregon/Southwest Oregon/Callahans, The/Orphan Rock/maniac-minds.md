# Description
Follows the NW corner arete starting just left (looking downhill) and downhill of the upper saddle ridge. Involves several layback moves with a crux move onto the summit.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, 2007 pp. 66-67, 152.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion bolts
