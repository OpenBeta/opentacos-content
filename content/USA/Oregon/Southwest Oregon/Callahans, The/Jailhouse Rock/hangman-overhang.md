# Description
Begins a few feet to the right of Slammer and the large madrone at the base of Jailhouse Rock.

Climbs into the chimney-like feature just left of the upper arete.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua map page 66-65, map 6 page 123.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion, no chains
