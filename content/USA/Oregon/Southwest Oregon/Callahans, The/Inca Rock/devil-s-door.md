# Description
A Short 15 foot ascent to a madrone and large ledge, then a scramble to the summit block above.

Rappel of top anchors, no chains.

# Location
Refer to Umpqua guide Callahan's map pp. 66 and photo pp. 152.

Begins on uphill saddle ridge between Inca and Orphan Rocks.

# Protection
bolts, no chains.
