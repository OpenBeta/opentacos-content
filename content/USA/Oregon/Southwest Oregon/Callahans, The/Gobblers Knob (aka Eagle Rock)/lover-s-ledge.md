# Description
Scramble class 4 out to top anchors out at the end of Gobblers Knob.

Rappel to the base of a 20 foot chimney crack and climb back.

# Location
Gobblers Knob.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua pages 171-172

# Protection
bolted top anchors
