# Description
About 20 feet past the start of Lover's Ledge you will come to a left leaning crack. Top anchors are located on top Gobblers Knob just before the gap in the summit ridge.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua map pages 71-72.

# Protection
bolted top anchors
