# Description
Find the bolted line near the bottom of the rock. P1: Climb up and left to a belay (65 ft). P2: Climb a short vertical crack to a ledge. Follow the ledge to a small 15 ft high dihedral. From the top of the dihedral go right and climb 10 feet into a cave. P3: From the cave climb an arete to the top. Rappel off bolts, no chains.

# Location
Downhill side of Gobblers Knob.

# Protection
bolts, no chains
