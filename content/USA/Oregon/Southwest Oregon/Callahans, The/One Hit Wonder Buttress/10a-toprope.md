# Description
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua 2007. photo page 152, topos Ai & Aj pages 164-165.

Top anchors mid way up the NE face can be accessed by climbing the first part of Electric Avenue or with a 60 foot rappel from the top anchors to Electric Ave.

Due to its NE aspect route may require maintenance at the beginning of each season

# Location
NE side of One Hit Wonder Buttress to the right of Electric Avenue.

top anchors
