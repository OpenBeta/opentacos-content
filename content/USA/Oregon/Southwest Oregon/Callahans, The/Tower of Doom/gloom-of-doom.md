# Description
Fun route when clean but requires maintenance of which it has gotten behind in.

Gloom of Doom and Doors of Durin climb the NE wall of Tower of Doom just uphill from the Baseline Trail just before it leaves the formation. Gloom begins about 20 feet uphill (right looking up) of Doors of Durin (5.9). Follow bolt line up steep slab to summit of Tower of Doom.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua map 7 128.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua map page 66-65, map 7 128, and GPS coordinates in UTM's page 127.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion, no chains.
