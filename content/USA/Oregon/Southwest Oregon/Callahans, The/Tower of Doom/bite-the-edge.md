# Description
Begins at lower left (SW) buttress corner of the Tower of Doom formation. From corner move uphill 10 feet and follow bolt line up the buttress and onto the face to the top of the SW buttress. Fun exposure and one of the lower maintenance routes. Top anchors can be reached by way of French Door (5.8).

# Location
SW buttress of Tower of Doom.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua map page 66-65, map 7 128, and GPS coordinates in UTM's page 127.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion, no chains
