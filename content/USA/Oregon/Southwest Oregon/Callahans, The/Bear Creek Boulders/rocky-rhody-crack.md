# Description
Twenty foot crack up right wall of Secrete Passage.

# Location
Right wall of Secret Passage uphill of the Bear Creek Road in the bouldering area.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua 2007, pg 173-174.

# Protection
gear 3/4 to 2 inches
