# Description
Steep 25 foot crack on right wall of Secrete Passage. In shadows and requires annual maintenance.

# Location
Secrete Passage of the bouldering area, uphill of Bear Creek Road.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua 2007, pg 173-174.

# Protection
gear 3/4 to 2 inches.
