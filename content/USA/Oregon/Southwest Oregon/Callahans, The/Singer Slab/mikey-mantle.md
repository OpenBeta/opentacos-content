# Description
Fun friction climbing where good mantling technique helps. After passing a vary large ledge, use friction and mantling to reach the top anchors.

# Location
Fifth route right of the left corner of Singer Slab.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua map 3a & 3b pages 104-105 and GPS coordinates in UTM's page 111.

# Protection
bolts and chains
