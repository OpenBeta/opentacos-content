# Description
climb lower head wall to large ledge (5.7) from here you have the option of continuing straight up over steep slightly overhung rock to the large ledge and belay anchors above (5.10a/b) or deviating right and up to the same top anchors (5.8). Rappel off, no chains.

# Location
South Wall just right of Space Cowboy.

# Protection
bolts, no chains
