# Description
follow bolts to belay anchors below the Looking-glass Crack and on top White Rabbit.

Rappel from anchors, no chains or continue up Through the Lookingglass (5.8).

# Location
Next bolt line past small ridge around the corner from Rocket Man on East Wall.

Bottom half of East Wall tends to hold moss if it goes unused for any length of time.

# Protection
bolts, no chains.
