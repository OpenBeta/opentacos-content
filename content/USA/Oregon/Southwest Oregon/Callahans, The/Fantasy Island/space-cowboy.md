# Description
Refer to Umpqua guide topo Z, pp.143.

Climb up lower head wall left of large overhang, then straight up to the a large ledge with belay anchors. Rappel off of anchors, no chains. Top anchor to Rocket Man 5.10b are within easy access to right (facing the rock).

# Location
Refer to Umpqua guide map 10, pp. 136.

Rappel from the SW ledge to the base of the South Wall or follow the scramble trail around the base of the lower wall to the base of the South Wall.

# Protection
Bolts, no chains.
