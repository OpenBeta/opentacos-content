# Description
Begins with a neat thin mantel and step up onto the mantel (5.10b). First bolt is above the mantel and is high. Stick clip helpful. The next 20 feet off the mantel move through steep thin friction 5.10 b/c). Follow bolt line tying in to right corner with top anchors on top of the ridge just right of the corner (5.6). No chains.

# Location
on the walkway 2nd route to left from corner crack. First is corner route.

# Protection
bolts, no chains on top anchors
