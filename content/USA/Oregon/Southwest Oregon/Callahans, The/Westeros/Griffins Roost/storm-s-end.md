# Description
From the saddle between Sunspear and Griffin's make the short but fun climb to the summit of Griffin's Roost (5.9+).

Rappel off, there may not be chains.

# Location
From the base of Sunspear Pinnacle hike the scramble trail uphill about 50 ft to the saddle and uphill backside of Griffin's

# Protection
bolted
