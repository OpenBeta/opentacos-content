# Description
Climbs the east leg of Griffin's Roost. Where the trail around the base of Griffin's Roost becomes narrowest scramble uphill to a bolt line that travels up the east leg. Crux is over steep slab in the center of the route.

# Location
East leg of Griffin's Roost of Westeros.

# Protection
bolts and chains
