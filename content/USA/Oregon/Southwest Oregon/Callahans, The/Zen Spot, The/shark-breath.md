# Description
Climb the bolted line up the left side corner of the rock as it rises off the ramp leading to Shark Rock. The crux is a dead-point of dyno to catch a hold onto the top.

# Location
Access from the uphill saddle leading out to Shark Rock.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua map pages 66-67, photo and map 13 pages 169-170. and GPS coordinates in UTM's page 169.

# Protection
bolts
