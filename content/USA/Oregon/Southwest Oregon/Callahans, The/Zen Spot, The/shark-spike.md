# Description
A bit of old Callahan history, this route is protected with fixed railroad spikes. As you climb synch a draw to each spike.

# Location
Sort northeast wall on uphill saddle of Shark Tooth Rock next to Shark Breath.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua map pages 66-67, photo and map 13 pages 169-170. and GPS coordinates in UTM's page 169.

# Protection
Fixed railroad spikes
