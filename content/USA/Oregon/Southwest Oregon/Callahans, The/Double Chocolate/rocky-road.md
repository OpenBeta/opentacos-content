# Description
The crux (5.7) of this climb is the first 10 feet.

Requires a brushing at the beginning of each season.

Easily toproped by way of Gold FInger (5.5).

Refer to  Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2 Umpqua 2007 Map 4 pp. 108.

# Location
Uphill west side of Double Chocolate.

Refer to  Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2 Umpqua 2007 Maps 3a, 3b, and 4 pp. 104-108.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion
