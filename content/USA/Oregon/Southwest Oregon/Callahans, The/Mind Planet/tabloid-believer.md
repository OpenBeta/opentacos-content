# Description
P1: Seventh bolt line to the left off starting of Denial Ledge. Begin by climbing through chossy, overhung rock pulling an awkward crux over a bulge onto a ledge and small cave. Follow the bolt line out of the cave onto better quality rock and follow bolted path of least resistance to share top anchors with Denying Denial.
