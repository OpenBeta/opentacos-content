# Description
Begins with a challenging roof pull off Denial Ledge up onto the vertical wall above following bolts to rappel anchors just short of the top. Crux moves are through a balancy slab sequence before weaving through amaze of angled huecos.

# Location
Sixth obvious bolt line as you walk out Denial Ledge from Relativity.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2 Umpqua. Map 1 page 80 and Topos D & E page 88, 90.

# Protection
bolts and chains.
