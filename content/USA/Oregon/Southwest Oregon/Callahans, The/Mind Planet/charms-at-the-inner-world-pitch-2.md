# Description
Pitch-1: Inside Passage (50ft., 5.10a/b).

Pitch-2 (45ft, 5.10c): From top anchors to pitch-1 continue to summit following bolts with a thin friction crux finish and lots of exposure. Walk-off.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Topo E page 90.

# Location
Far left side of Denial Ledge.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Map 1 page 80 and Topo E page 90.

# Protection
bolts and chains
