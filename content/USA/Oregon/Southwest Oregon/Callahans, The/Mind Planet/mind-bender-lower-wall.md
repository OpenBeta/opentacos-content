# Description
Begins at the Baseline Trail and follows the only bolt line up the lower wall to Denial Ledge. Steep thin friction moves followed by a climb over two bulges. Rappel, walk-off Denial Ledge, of continue onto pitches-2 & 3 up Denial Wall to summit (130 ft., 5.12c).

The lower wall is in tree shadows it requires annual maintenance at the beginning of each season.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Topo E page 90.

# Location
Baseline Trail below Denial Ledge.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Map 1 page 80 and Topo E page 90.

# Protection
bolts and chains
