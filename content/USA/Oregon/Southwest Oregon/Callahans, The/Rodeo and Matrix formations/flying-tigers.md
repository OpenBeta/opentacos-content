# Description
Climb the seam located 10 to 15 feet to the right of Bull Roarer. At the top of the seam climb right around the overhanging wall to the summit. If you are the first ascent of the season expect to do some route maintenance. Easily toproped after climbing Bull Roarer (5.7).

# Location
Uphill side of Rodeo Rock.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon Vol. 2: Umpqua 2007. Topo C page 88.

# Protection
Fixe SS 3/8 double expansion, no chains.
