# Description
Shares bottom belay anchors with Animal Quackers. Scramble up 20 feet to large blocks, traverse right (looking up) to a crack and follow the crack to summit belay anchors with a slab move at the end.

Needs cleaning.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Topo Ag page 161.
