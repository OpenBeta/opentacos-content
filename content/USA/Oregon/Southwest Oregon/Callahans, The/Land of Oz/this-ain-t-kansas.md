# Description
Same start on Y-crack as Horse of a Different Color. Climbs the right arm of the Y-crack with a lay-back crux move 3/4 of the way up.

Rappel or walk-off.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Topo Ab page 148.

# Location
Right side of center buttress at Land of Oz.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Map 11 page 147 and Topo Ab page 148.

# Protection
gear tp 4 inches, top anchors and chains
