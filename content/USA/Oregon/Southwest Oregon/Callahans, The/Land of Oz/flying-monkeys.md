# Description
Climbs past three steep huecos then either dyno right to a large bucket of make a clever static move to get past the third bolt (5.10d). Easier climbing up jugs leads to 5.10a friction to chains.

# Location
Furthest bolt line to the right side of Land of Oz.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Map 11 page 147.

# Protection
bolts and chains
