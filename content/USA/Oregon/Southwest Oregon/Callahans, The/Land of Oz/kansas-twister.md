# Description
From the top of Wicked Witch (5.10a) rappel back side (north) of the ridge to a ledge and large Douglas-fir (most likely a stump now). Follow bolts back up to the ridge top anchors.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Map 11 page 147.
