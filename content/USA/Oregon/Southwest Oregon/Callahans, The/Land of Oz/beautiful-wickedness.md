# Description
Sustained pumpy overhung moves. An easy start the quickley becomes overhung. At pockets about 3/4 of the way up veer left for a tricky sequence near the corner, then right for a final tough move.

# Location
2nd bolted line to the left from the right side of Land of Oz.

Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua Map 11 page 147.

# Protection
bolts and chains
