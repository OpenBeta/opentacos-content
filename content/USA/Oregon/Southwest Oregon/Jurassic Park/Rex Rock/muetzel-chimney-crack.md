# Description
Climb east wall on the T-Rex formation starting to the right of the Muetzel Chimney, moving into the chimney as it opens up. This route is in need of cleaning and bolting.

# Location
Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Vol. 2: Umpqua 2007 maps pages 194, 196.

# Protection
one 1/4 high carbon steel bolts plus gear of unknown size
