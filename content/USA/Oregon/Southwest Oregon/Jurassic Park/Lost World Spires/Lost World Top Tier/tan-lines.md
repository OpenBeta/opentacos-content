# Description
90' wall with a clean finger crack starting from a ledge about 40' up.  Bottom of climb is face climbing, needs some cleaning.  Strenuous finger crack leads to a small cave and a boulder problem out to the top.

# Location
Scramble uphill to the top of the rocks.  Anchors located on western edge of the top tier.

# Protection
Anchors at top, easy toprope setup.
