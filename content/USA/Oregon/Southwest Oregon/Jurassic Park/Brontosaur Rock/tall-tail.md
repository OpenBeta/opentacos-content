# Description
Begin at road or scramble up road cut and belay from two bolts ontop of the road cut. Follow bolts to the top of the first prominent summit. End to the route is obvious because you would need to rappel to continue any further. First pit is 150 feet, 2nd is about 50 feet. Rappel from webbing down the way you came up using two ropes on your 2nd rappel. Route can use cleaning and chains.

# Location
Start from the road of on top the road cut.

# Protection
3/8 inch SS Fixe Double expansion. 1" tubuaalr webbing at rappels.
