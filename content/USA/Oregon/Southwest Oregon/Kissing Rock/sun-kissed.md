# Description
Starts up ridgeline 40 ft right of First Kiss. Begins with a 40 foot class IV scramble to first bolt. Crux (5.8) climbs through last two bolts near the top. Refer to Rock Climbing Western Oregon, Rogue, 2005, West Face Kissing Rock topo pp. 172.

# Location
Southwest Face 40 ft left of First Kiss.

# Protection
5 bolts and top anchor bolts. quality unknown
