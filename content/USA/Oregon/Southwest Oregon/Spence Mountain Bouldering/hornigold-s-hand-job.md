# Description
Walking to the right of the boulder locate the solid but very detached flake.  Hands start on the flake and feet to the downhill side facilitate the pull into an undercling and a full pad rail.  Find the feet and pull to the top.

# Location
Right of the approach trail.  Look for bomber detached flake.
